<h1 id="matesignal">MateSignal</h1>
<p><strong>MateSignal</strong> is a Forge <strong>client-side mod</strong> that enables cross-interaction with <strong>MateEngine</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>MateEngine</strong> is a 3D Desktop Avatar Application that lets you display your favorite 3D or VTuber models on your PC.<br />It includes features like <strong>dancing</strong>, <strong>window sitting</strong>, and many other fun interactions.<br />This mod acts as a <strong>bridge between Minecraft and MateEngine</strong>.</p>
<hr />
<h3 id="what-it-does">What it does</h3>
<p>During various in-game events such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Crafting</li>
<li>Weather changes</li>
<li>Nearby mobs</li>
<li>Sleeping</li>
<li>Day/Night cycle changes</li>
<li>Low health or hunger</li>
</ul>
<p>&hellip;the avatar will display <strong>cute messages on your desktop</strong>.<br />For example, when you go to sleep in Minecraft, the 3D mascot will cheerfully say <em>&ldquo;Good night!&rdquo;</em>.</p>
<hr />
<h3 id="usage-warning">Usage Warning</h3>
<p>Do <strong>not</strong> use this mod on public or competitive servers.<br />The avatar can say things like <em>&ldquo;Heyyy!! There&rsquo;s a creeper behind you!!&rdquo;</em> which might be considered <strong>cheating</strong> on some servers.<br />Use this mod <strong>only</strong> in <strong>singleplayer</strong> or <strong>private multiplayer</strong> worlds with friends.</p>
<hr />
<h3 id="technical-note">Technical Note</h3>
<p>If the mod doesn&rsquo;t work:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure <strong>port 32145</strong> is open in your firewall.</li>
<li>Make sure <strong>TCP and UDP</strong> traffic is properly forwarded.<br />The mod runs a <strong>local server</strong> on your local IP to communicate securely with MateEngine.</li>
</ul>
<hr />
<h3 id="additional-info">Additional Info</h3>
<p>MateEngine itself is written in <strong>C#</strong> using the <strong>Unity Engine</strong>, and it is <strong>open-source and free</strong>.<br />(Just in case any CurseForge staff gets worried about &ldquo;advertising,&rdquo; don&rsquo;t worry &mdash; it&rsquo;s a free project.)</p>
